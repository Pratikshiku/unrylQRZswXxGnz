## Agricultural Product Smart Logistics System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/620a89ce3ee940049445e377216fb5bf/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, agricultural product suppliers, agricultural product logistics companies, administrators.

**Regular User Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Forum
5. Agricultural Product Suppliers
6. Agricultural Products
7. Agricultural Product Commission
8. Agricultural Product Logistics Companies
9. Personal Center
10. Delivery Address
11. Agricultural Product Collection
12. Agricultural Product Reviews
13. Agricultural Product Orders
14. Agricultural Product Delivery
15. Shopping Cart


**Agricultural Product Supplier Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Agricultural Product Commission Management
6. Agricultural Product Delivery Management
7. Forum Management
8. Announcement Information Management


**Agricultural Product Logistics Company Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Agricultural Product Commission Management
6. Agricultural Product Delivery Management
7. Forum Management
8. Announcement Information Management




**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Agricultural Product Supplier Management
5. Agricultural Product Logistics Company Management
6. User Management
7. Agricultural Product Commission Management
8. Agricultural Product Delivery Management
9. Agricultural Product Management
10. Agricultural Product Review Management
11. Agricultural Product Collection Management
12. Agricultural Product Order Management
13. Basic Data Management (Supplier Type Management, Announcement Type Management, Commission Type Management, Logistics Company Type Management, Agricultural Product Type Management)
14. Forum Management
15. Announcement Information Management
16. Carousel Information
